Media Briefing

South Africa has the largest antiretroviral treatment program in the world.

Africa Check Feb 8, 2024

President Ramaphosa's 2024 SONA address claims on economy, jobs, and social grants.

Africa Check found three claims incorrect, two understated, three misleading, and three correct regarding the 2024 State of the Nation Address. For example, Ramaphosa overstated the number of social grant beneficiaries by over six million people by mistaking grants for recipients.

Africa Check Nov 14, 2019

Fact-Checked: President Ramaphosa's claims about progress in South Africa since 1994.

While Ramaphosa was correct about the economy and electrified households, his claims on university enrollment were not fully supported and data on inequality was hard to prove.

Africa Check Feb 20, 2018

Ramaphosa right about 'big difference' between black & white unemployment in SA.

In response to concerns about white youth exclusion, Ramaphosa correctly stated that unemployment among Africans was 30% and just under 7% for white people in Q4 2017.
