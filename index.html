Our Verification Process

Transparency is key to accountability. Our methodology ensures rigorous and impartial analysis of every presidential statement.

1. Source Identification

We identify original sources (transcripts, official documents, video) to verify claims against primary evidence.

2. Expert Data Analysis

Specialist fact-checkers and policy analysts evaluate data, context, and statistics from credible public bodies.

3. Final Rating & Publication

A clear, final rating is assigned, with a detailed breakdown of the evidence to ensure full transparency.
